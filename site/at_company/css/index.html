1. ROUND PIC

How To Turn a rectangle pic into a round one:

    .circular--landscape{
        display:inline-block;
        position:relative;
        width:200px;
        height:200px;
        overflow:hidden;
        border-radius:50%;
    }

    .circular--landscape img{
        width:auto;
        height:100%;
        margin-left:-50%;
    }
